Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026

1. Overview

ClipForge is an AI-powered video automation platform designed to create and manage short-form video content. We respect user privacy and are committed to protecting data in accordance with applicable laws and platform policies.

2. Data Usage

ClipForge only processes data necessary to provide its core functionality, including:

  • Video content provided by the user
  • Metadata such as titles, descriptions, and thumbnails
  • Content publishing data when interacting with external platforms

No personal data is collected or processed beyond what is required for the intended video creation and publishing workflow.

3. Data Storage

ClipForge does not store user data permanently unless explicitly required for processing workflows. Temporary data may be used during processing and is handled securely.

4. Third-Party Services

ClipForge may interact with third-party APIs (such as TikTok) strictly within the scope of authorized actions. No unauthorized data access or sharing occurs.

5. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect data against unauthorized access or misuse.

6. User Control

Users retain full control over their content and may revoke access or permissions at any time through platform settings.
