Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Introduction

ClipForge ("we", "our", or "us") is a short-form video publishing tool that helps creators and businesses prepare and share video content on supported platforms. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our service.

By using ClipForge, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account information – information provided when you authorize ClipForge through a supported platform's official login flow (e.g., TikTok OAuth).
  • Content data – video files, captions, descriptions, and related metadata that you provide for publishing purposes.
  • Usage data – basic information about how you interact with the service, such as actions taken and session information.

We do not collect more data than is necessary to provide the core functionality of ClipForge.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the ClipForge service
  • Publish content to connected platform accounts on your behalf
  • Respond to support requests
  • Improve the service over time

We do not sell your personal information to third parties.

4. Platform Integrations

ClipForge integrates with third-party platforms such as TikTok through their official APIs and authorization mechanisms. When you connect a platform account, ClipForge only requests the permissions needed to perform the actions you authorize. You can revoke access at any time through your platform account settings.

5. Data Storage and Retention

ClipForge does not permanently store your video content or personal data beyond what is required for active processing workflows. Temporary data used during processing is handled securely and discarded after use.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, but we take reasonable steps to protect your data.

7. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal information. To exercise these rights, or if you have questions about how your data is handled, please contact us using the details below.

You may also revoke ClipForge's access to your connected platform accounts at any time through those platforms' account settings.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will post the revised policy on this page with an updated date. Continued use of ClipForge after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
