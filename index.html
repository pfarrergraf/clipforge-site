Short-Form Video Publishing

What ClipForge Does

ClipForge is a web-based tool designed to help content creators and businesses manage their short-form video workflow. It simplifies the process of preparing, organizing, and publishing video content to supported platforms, including TikTok.

Users connect their accounts through official platform authorization flows. ClipForge only accesses the data and permissions that the user explicitly grants.

How It Works

  1. 1
    Connect your account Authorize ClipForge through the official TikTok login flow. You stay in control of your permissions at all times.
  2. 2
    Prepare your content Upload or select video content, add captions or descriptions, and review your post before publishing.
  3. 3
    Publish Send your content to your connected account directly from ClipForge. You can revoke access from your platform settings at any time.
